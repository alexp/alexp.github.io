zenblogs.co
Dockerised Ghost blogs

One click Ghost blogging platform install and hosting.

I've fiddled with this sideproject throughout a couple of weekends.
It lets you get your own, free instance of Ghost and start blogging right away.

The service works on Docker, spawning a new container per blog created.

Technologies used:

  • Docker
  • Ruby On Rails
  • Postgreql
  • Unicorn
  • Nginx
  • jQuery
